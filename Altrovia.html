By Karen Zheng, Olivia Jung, Jane Zhou

Altrovia AI

The original Altrovia website left users unsure about the product’s purpose and workflow. Messaging was vague, and the experience lacked concrete examples of how Altrovia AI supports clinical trial design. We focused on clarity, a tighter information hierarchy, and stronger visual storytelling to make the platform feel both useful and credible.

Problem

Altrovia wasn't clear
about its value.

The original site did not clearly communicate Altrovia AI’s value or how it fits into a researcher’s workflow. Users needed faster access to the product’s core capabilities and practical use cases.

The redesign made the product narrative more direct while also showcasing interface screens so visitors can immediately understand how Altrovia supports trial planning and analysis.

Solution

Clarify the product
and show how it works.

We rephrased website descriptions to make Altrovia AI’s functionality clear and concise. We also incorporated a concrete visual of the interface so viewers can see what designing a clinical trial looks like, rather than just reading about it.
